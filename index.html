Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our menu, select your items, and click "Order Now" to place your order via WhatsApp. We'll confirm and prepare your meal fresh.

What are your delivery hours?

We are open from 11:00 AM to 11:00 PM, seven days a week. Orders placed after 10:30 PM will be delivered the next day.

Do you offer custom orders?

Yes! Contact us via WhatsApp with your custom order requirements, and we'll do our best to accommodate your preferences.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery, credit/debit cards, and digital payments via JazzCash and EasyPaisa.
